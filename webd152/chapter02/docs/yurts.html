Yurts at Pacific Trails Resort

Frequently asked questions

What is a Yurt?
A yurt is a round tent-like structure with a wooden frame and a fabric covering. It is a traditional dwelling used by nomadic people, but now modernized for a luxurious camping experience.
Are the Yurts heated?
Yes, all our yurts come with heating systems to ensure comfort, even during colder nights.
Can I bring pets to the Yurts?
Unfortunately, we do not allow pets in the yurts to maintain a peaceful and clean environment for all guests.
How do I book a Yurt?
You can make a reservation by visiting our reservation page or calling us directly at 888-555-1212.
